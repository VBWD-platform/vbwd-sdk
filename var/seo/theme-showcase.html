What browsers are supported?

All evergreen browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). IE11 is not supported.

Can I self-host?

Yes — every piece is Apache-2.0 and ships as Docker images.

How do payments work?

Stripe by default; PayPal and YooKassa ship as plugins. You can write your own adapter in about 80 lines.
