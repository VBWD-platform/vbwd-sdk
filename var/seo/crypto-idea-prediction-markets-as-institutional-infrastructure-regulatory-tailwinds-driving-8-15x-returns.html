  • Strategy Explanation

    Prediction markets enable price discovery for uncertain events (elections, economic outcomes, geopolitical scenarios) through decentralized consensus. Unlike traditional derivatives, blockchain-native prediction markets offer 24/7 settlement, reduced counterparty risk, and transparent order books. Institutional adoption drivers: (1) Risk management – enterprises hedge macro scenarios without traditional derivatives counterparty exposure; (2) Scenario planning – corporations use prediction market pricing for strategic forecasting; (3) Portfolio hedging – macro funds gain uncorrelated alpha from prediction market positions. Why it matters: $3T+ derivatives market shows institutional demand for uncertainty pricing. Prediction markets capture this demand with lower operational friction and regulatory clarity than legacy infrastructure.
