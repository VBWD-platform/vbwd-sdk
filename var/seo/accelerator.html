A typical week-one timeline

  1. Day 1 — Fork vbwd-demo-instances, point DNS at your VPS, run setup.sh. Stack boots.
  2. Day 2 — Pick the vertical closest to your business, disable plugins you don't need, enable ones you do.
  3. Day 3 — Replace the CMS home page, about, contact. Swap the theme colour in docs/imports/theme-styles.json.
  4. Day 4 — Wire your Stripe / PayPal account in the admin. Create your real products / rooms / appointments.
  5. Day 5 — Run the Playwright smoke tests against your domain. Fix what breaks.
  6. Day 6–7 — Soft launch to a pilot group.
