About VBWD

VBWD is an open-source, self-hosted subscription platform built by a developer, for developers. We believe subscription infrastructure should be something you own — not rent, not share with a vendor, and not pay a percentage of your revenue to access. So we built it, tested it thoroughly, and released it to the public domain.

What We Do

We build and maintain the VBWD platform — a complete SaaS billing and subscriber management kit you deploy on your own server. It ships with a Python/Flask API, a Vue 3 user-facing storefront, and a Vue 3 admin backoffice, all wired together and ready to run with a single command. Subscription plans, token economy, add-ons, invoicing, multi-currency payments, a plugin system, CMS, AI chat, and embeddable pricing widgets — production-ready, 1,851 tests passing.

Our Story

VBWD started as a frustration. Every time we needed to launch a subscription product, we faced the same choice: pay Stripe 0.5–0.8% of revenue forever, hand Substack 10% of every subscriber dollar, or spend months building billing infrastructure from scratch. None of those options made sense for a solo developer or a small team.

So we built what we needed: a self-hosted platform with no transaction fees, no vendor lock-in, and no ceiling on what you can do with it. The plugin system came from a second frustration — vertical SaaS products always required forking the core and maintaining diverging codebases. VBWD separates core (agnostic) from plugins (gnostic), so you extend without breaking anything.

Today VBWD supports eight payment gateways, has plugins for AI chat, CMS, tarot readings, analytics, Stripe, PayPal, and YooKassa, and is used to power coaching platforms, newsletter businesses, creator economies, and agency billing tools across Europe and beyond.

Why Self-Hosted?

  • Zero platform fees — Substack takes 10%. Gumroad takes 10%. OnlyFans takes 20%. VBWD takes nothing. You pay for your VPS (€12/month) and keep everything else.
  • EU Data Act & GDPR compliance by architecture — Your data lives on your server, in your jurisdiction. No US CLOUD Act exposure. No vendor DPA needed. Compliant by default, not by contract.
  • No vendor lock-in, ever — VBWD is CC0 (public domain). You own the source code unconditionally. If we disappear tomorrow, your business keeps running.
  • Full control — You decide which features exist via the plugin system. No feature requests going into a backlog. No waiting for a SaaS vendor to prioritize your niche.

Our Values

  • Ownership — Software you run on your own server is software you truly control. We design for operators, not for retention metrics.
  • Zero hidden costs — No transaction fees, no per-seat pricing, no enterprise tiers gatekeeping core features. The platform is CC0. Infrastructure is your only cost.
  • Agnostic core, gnostic plugins — The core platform never knows your business. Your business logic lives in plugins, isolated and replaceable, without touching the foundation.
  • Quality over velocity — 1,851 tests across backend, frontend, and E2E. We do not ship features without coverage. Stability is a feature.
  • Transparency — The entire codebase is public. The architecture decisions are documented in the devlog. There are no hidden services, no telemetry, no callbacks home.

License

VBWD is released under CC0 1.0 Universal (Public Domain Dedication). This means you can copy, modify, distribute, and use the software for any purpose — commercial or non-commercial — without asking permission, without attribution, and without any legal friction. It is the most permissive license that exists. We chose it deliberately, because we believe infrastructure should be free in every sense of the word.
