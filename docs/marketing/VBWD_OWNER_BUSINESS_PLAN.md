# VBWD Owner Business Plan & Cash Flow Projection

**Date:** March 2026
**Role:** Solo founder / platform owner
**Product:** VBWD-SDK — BSL 1.1 licensed self-hosted SaaS subscription platform
**Goal:** Build a sustainable software business through direct sales + agency partner channel

---

## The Owner's Perspective

The agency opportunities document describes how agencies make money WITH VBWD. This document describes how YOU — the owner of VBWD — make money FROM agencies, direct customers, and the ecosystem you build around the platform.

Every agency deployment, every direct customer, every partner — they all feed back to you through license fees, hosting revenue, partner commissions on payment providers, and custom development. You're building a platform business, not a services business.

## Agency Partner Economics — Your Side

This is the engine that scales without your direct involvement.

### How It Works

1. Agency signs up as partner (free or paid tier)
2. Agency sells VBWD solution to their client
3. Agency buys license through your partner portal
4. You fulfill the license, agency earns commission
5. If the client wants managed hosting → you host, agency may or may not be involved
6. Payment provider activations → you earn cashback, split with agency

### Projection: Partner Network Growth

| Month | Active Partners | Licenses via Partners (cumulative) | Your Revenue (net) |
|-------|----------------|------------------------------------|--------------------|
| 3 | 1 | 1 | $374 |
| 6 | 3 | 6 | $4,120 |
| 9 | 6 | 15 | $11,368 |
| 12 | 10 | 28 | $21,598 |
| 18 | 18 | 60 | $52,000 |
| 24 | 25 | 105 | $96,000 |

### Partner Lifetime Value (to You)

Average agency partner:
- Sells 8 licenses/year → your net: $8,992/year (avg $1,124 net per Professional license)
- 3 of those clients buy hosting from you → $5,364/year
- 2 clients need custom plugin work, referred to you → $12,000/year
- Payment cashback from partner's deployments → $300/year
- **Partner LTV to you: ~$26,656/year per active agency partner**

**10 active partners = $266,560/year in partner-driven revenue.** This is why building the partner channel is the highest-leverage activity after product development.
